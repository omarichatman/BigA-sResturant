Mission Statement

"Big A's Concessions" is owned and operated by N. Kuykendall Enterprises, LLC. It's name is a tribute to my mother, Alice Bennett who dies November 14, 2014 at 90 year sof age. After closing "Bennett's Market and Deli" in mid 1990's, she yearned for a new business to leave her legacy and created self-esteem to the 72 Grand and Great-grandchildren she left behind. This tribute can never reflect her life worthiness, it is only a tiny contribution to the continued sacrifices she made during her life. Big A's Concessions, will continue to reflect love and affection as of its legacy and promises to provide safe and delicious foods and the highest quality of service to all customers.
